About Me

Hey there, I'm Don! I'm a easy-going guy that is passionate about technology and passionate about teaching people about technology.

I got my start teaching in the spring of 2005 working for Macomb Community College in Warren, Michigan. It was there that I worked for MCC's "Workforce Development Institute", which provided customized training programs for business, industry and government.

After being laid off from MCC (and soon afterwards an engineering position) I decided to go back to school at MCC under Michigan's "No Worker Left Behind" program. I am currently pursuing my degree in network security (another thing that I am passionate about) and I am on track to graduate in March of 2011.
